Semilogx Bar Plot for MATLAB

I found it extremely inconvenient without this functionality sometimes, so I decided to just write one myself. Help info is contained in the .m file. Given proper input, it produces bar plot on semilogx axis with given center points and values. “Proper” means you’ll have to ensure the input are approximately equal-spaced in the log domain, or the output may look weird. Here’s what it looks like. 
>> x=[1 2 4 8 16];
>> y=rand(1,5);
>> semilogxBar(x,y)

Download semilogxBar.m
