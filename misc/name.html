My Name (Mostly Pronunciation)

It came to my understanding that my name could be hard to think of a pronunciation for for most native speakers in English.

Although I personally have totally no problems with English speakers pronouncing my name as “P EH NG K IY” (ARPAbet notation), and that’s what I also self-introduce my name as, a more close-to-original pronunciation is something like “P AX NG CH IY”.

Should anyone be curious, the Chinese characters are “齐鹏” (Surname first), and pronounced in Mandarin as “tɕʰi2 pʰəŋ2” in IPA notation (or “qi2 peng2” in Pinyin).
