Me & My Camera

I take pictures as I move around the world.

I am also a fan of movies, and always dreamt of directing movies of my own. My first attempt as a movie director, AI.恋 (A.I.’s Love), showed up in my undergraduate department’s Students’ Festival Gala (学生节). I also edited a documentary, 拾年 (Ten Years), that reviewed how my undergraduate department came into being. I also wrote the screenplay for the graduation DV, 梦见 (Dream), which was shown at the Students’ Festival Gala and was a great success. Unfortunately, all of these are in Chinese and none has an English subtitle.
