Me & My Homepage

I am recently quite interested in Twitter Bootstrap, with which I designed the template of this page. Hope that this template can be used by more people, as I designed it in a quite open style for almost anyone.

It is easy if you want to use the template without changing the appearance of it, but after reading some quick tutorials / hacks that I plan to write, changing the look will be easy as pie. Besides, I plan to update the hyde source files of this template for better readability and reusability.

From this day on, any form of reuse of the hyde source files is permitted given that the “Designed by Qi Peng / Powered by hyde.” logo remains in each page (Qi Peng is the Chinese ordering of my name). However it is your freedom of will to change its color and size as long as it’s still recognizable.

Please bookmark this very page or my news page for the latest updates of this template or related tutorials / hacks.

  • You may have noticed that some (in fact a lot) images on this site have a tiny blue top right corner like this . This awesome functionality is powered by FancyBox and jQuery, and the implementation will be introduced in Tutorial 1: The Fancy Corner.
