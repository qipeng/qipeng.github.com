Biography

I am currently a fourth-year bachelor student at School of Software, Tsinghua University, Beijing.

My research interest covers machine learning, especially deep learning, in which I seek innovation and inspiration from the essence of human being's intelligence. I also dabble in data mining, computer graphics and data visualization. I'm also interested in psycology and stuff. I am familiar with programing languages including but not limited to C/C++, Java, C#, MATLAB, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; and I'm recently learning Python, HTML5, CSS3 and other useful languages. I participated in many course projects, research projects and real-world projects during my undergraduate study (see Undergraduate Projects).
