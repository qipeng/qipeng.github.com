Brief Bio

I am currently a Master's student at the Computer Science Departmentof Stanford University. Here I'm a research assistant in Professor Andrew Ng's group.

My research interests broadly cover topics in machine learning, especially deep learning, with which I seek to solve real-world problems more effectively in applications including computer vision, speech recognition, and natural languages. I also dabble in GPGPU programming, computer graphics, data mining and data visualization.
